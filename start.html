FLLTutorials Tournament Scoring System


The administrator of the tournament will have to sign in to a Google account to set up the tournament.

During your login process, you will get a pop-up saying “This app isn’t verified”. Click on the small “advanced” link in the bottom left and then click "Go to flltutorials.com (unsafe)”

In the next dialog - click the “Allow” button. This tournament system will be creating spreadsheets for storing scores and sharing them with the other referees.

After you sign in, pick a tournament name. If you want to access an existing tournament you have made, type in the name of your tournament. If you have forgotten your tournament name, go to Tournaments List in the menu bar.
